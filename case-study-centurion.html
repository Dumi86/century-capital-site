Centurion Mobile Case Study

A radical departure from standard financial interfaces, reimagining high-net-worth asset management through a purely gestural, minimalist lens.

The Brief

Redefining high-net-worth mobile UX.

"The modern investor doesn't need more data—they need more clarity. Eliminate the clutter and create an extension of their intuition."

Century Capital's clients operate in a world where time is the ultimate currency. Traditional banking apps are cluttered with tabs, buttons, and notifications. Centurion Mobile was conceived to break this mold. Our mandate was to create a "Dark Room" experience: an interface that stays hidden until called upon, using gestures as the primary language of interaction.

Strategy 01

Tactile Invisibility

Removing all UI chrome to focus on the numbers that matter.

Strategy 02

Neon Precision

Using #775a19 sparingly to guide the eye through complex asset structures.

Strategy 03

Kinetic Flow

Mapping wealth growth to fluid, organic motion design.
