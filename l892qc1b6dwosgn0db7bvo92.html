Apex
Materials

Forging a premium brand identity for a leader in industrial and architectural materials — from raw to refined.

Project Summary

Apex Materials needed to differentiate in a commoditised market. They came to us wanting a brand that felt as premium as the materials they supplied.

ServicesBrand Identity
DeliverablesLogo · Guidelines · Kit
Duration6 Weeks
Result+180% Enquiries
IndustryArchitecture

Logo Mark Design

The Challenge

Industrial suppliers rarely invest in design — which is exactly why it creates opportunity. We built a visual language borrowing from architectural drawings: measured, technical, authoritative.

  • 01.Competing against established international brands with centuries of heritage.
  • 02.Needing to appeal to both high-end architects and procurement managers.
  • 03.Building trust for a relatively young company in a conservative industry.

Results

180%

Increase in inbound enquiries within 3 months of brand launch.

3x

Growth in average project contract value.

The Outcome

Apex Materials now carries itself as a premium, design-forward enterprise. The brand system has been applied across digital, print, signage and fleet — consistently and powerfully.
